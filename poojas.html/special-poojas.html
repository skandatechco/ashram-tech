Temple Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a peaceful and respectful worship experience for all devotees.

Arrival Time

Please arrive 15 minutes before the scheduled pooja time. Late arrivals may not be able to participate in certain rituals.

Dress Code

Traditional or modest clothing is preferred. Please avoid shorts, sleeveless tops, and revealing attire.

Mobile Phones

Please keep mobile phones on silent mode during worship services. Photography is allowed with permission.

Offerings

Flowers, fruits, and coconuts are welcome offerings. All offerings will be blessed and returned as prasadam.
