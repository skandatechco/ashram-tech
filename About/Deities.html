Sacred Deities

Discover the divine forms that bless our temple. Each deity represents unique aspects of the divine, offering guidance, protection, and spiritual fulfillment to all devotees.

Lord Murugan All Deities

Our Presiding Deity

Lord Murugan, the divine son of Lord Shiva, is the presiding deity of our temple. Known as Shanmukha (six-faced) and Kartikeya, he represents wisdom, courage, and spiritual victory.

🔱

Lord Murugan

Shanmukha • Kartikeya • Skanda

The eternal youth, commander of divine armies, and the embodiment of divine knowledge. Lord Murugan blesses devotees with wisdom, removes obstacles, and guides them on the path of righteousness.

Divine Attributes:

  • Six faces representing divine wisdom
  • Protector of devotees
  • Bestower of knowledge
  • Remover of sins and obstacles

Sacred Symbols:

  • 🦚Peacock (Divine vehicle)
  • 🔱Vel (Sacred spear)
  • 🐓Rooster (Victory flag)
  • 🌺Red lotus (Purity)

Worship Timings

Morning Pooja: 6:00 AM - 8:00 AM
Afternoon Pooja: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
Evening Pooja: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Sacred Mantra:

"Om Saravanabhavaya Namaha"
"Om Shanmukhaya Namaha"

Preferred Offerings

Lord Murugan is pleased with simple yet devotional offerings made with pure heart and sincere devotion.

Vel-shaped sweets Panchamirtham Red flowers Coconut Fruits Milk Honey Vibhuti

Shanmukha - The Six Faces

Each of Lord Murugan's six faces represents a different aspect of divine consciousness and spiritual wisdom.

👁️

Divine Vision

Sees beyond the material world into spiritual realms

🧠

Supreme Wisdom

Embodies all knowledge and understanding

💪

Divine Power

Possesses unlimited strength to protect devotees

❤️

Compassion

Shows infinite love and mercy to all beings

⚖️

Divine Justice

Upholds dharma and cosmic order

🕊️

Peace

Grants inner peace and spiritual tranquility

Other Sacred Deities

Our temple is blessed with the presence of multiple divine forms, each offering unique blessings and spiritual guidance to devotees.

🐘

Lord Ganesha

Vighna Harta • Remover of Obstacles

The beloved elephant-headed deity who removes obstacles and blesses new beginnings. Devotees seek his blessings before starting any important endeavor.

Sacred Mantra:

"Om Gam Ganapataye Namaha"

Offerings:
Modak Laddu Durva grass
🌺

Goddess Durga

Divine Mother • Protector

The divine mother who protects her devotees from all forms of evil and negativity. She embodies feminine power and divine grace.

Sacred Mantra:

"Om Dum Durgayei Namaha"

Offerings:
Red flowers Sindoor Sweets
🔱

Lord Shiva

Mahadeva • The Supreme

The supreme deity, destroyer of evil and transformer of consciousness. Father of Lord Murugan and the ultimate source of divine energy.

Sacred Mantra:

"Om Namah Shivaya"

Offerings:
Bilva leaves Milk Vibhuti
🪷

Goddess Lakshmi

Goddess of Wealth • Prosperity

The goddess of wealth, prosperity, and abundance. She blesses devotees with material and spiritual prosperity.

Sacred Mantra:

"Om Shreem Mahalakshmyai Namaha"

Offerings:
Lotus flowers Gold coins Sweets
🐒

Lord Hanuman

Devotee Supreme • Strength

The epitome of devotion, courage, and strength. He protects devotees from negative energies and grants physical and mental strength.

Sacred Mantra:

"Om Hanumate Namaha"

Offerings:
Sindoor Bananas Besan laddu
⭐

Navagraha

Nine Planetary Deities

The nine planetary deities who influence human destiny. Devotees worship them for planetary peace and astrological remedies.

Sacred Mantra:

"Om Navagraha Devaya Namaha"

Offerings:
Nine grains Colored flowers Oil lamps

Worship Guidelines

Follow these simple guidelines to make your temple visit and worship experience more meaningful and spiritually fulfilling.

Before Entering Temple

  • Remove shoes at the entrance
  • Wash hands and feet
  • Dress modestly and appropriately
  • Turn off mobile phones
  • Maintain peaceful mindset

During Worship

  • Offer prayers with devotion
  • Chant mantras silently
  • Make offerings respectfully
  • Maintain silence during ceremonies
  • Follow priest's instructions
