The Foundation Story

Every great journey begins with a vision. Discover how Skanda Sramam was born from the devotion and determination of a small group of Lord Murugan devotees.

The Vision Begins

In 1998, a small group of Tamil families in the community felt a deep calling to create a sacred space dedicated to Lord Murugan. What started as weekly gatherings in homes soon grew into a vision for a permanent temple.

The founding members, led by their unwavering faith and commitment to preserving Tamil culture and Hindu traditions, embarked on a journey that would transform not just their own lives, but the lives of thousands of devotees.

Started with 12 founding families
First gatherings in family homes
United by devotion to Lord Murugan

"We dreamed of creating not just a temple, but a spiritual home where future generations could connect with their roots, find peace, and serve the community with love."

- Founding Committee, 1998
