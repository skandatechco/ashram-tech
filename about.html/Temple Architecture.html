Construction Journey

The development of Skanda Sramam has been a journey of faith, community effort, and divine grace spanning over two decades.

1999 - Foundation & Planning

Initial planning phase with architectural consultations, Vastu compliance studies, and community fundraising efforts.

Design Phase

2001 - Ground Breaking

Sacred ground breaking ceremony followed by foundation laying with proper Vedic rituals and blessings.

Construction Start

2003 - Main Structure

Completion of main temple structure including sanctum, prayer hall, and basic facilities using traditional techniques.

Structure Complete

2005 - Consecration

Grand consecration ceremony (Kumbhabhishekam) establishing the divine presence and opening for worship.

Temple Opening

2010 - Cultural Center

Addition of cultural center, expanded kitchen facilities, and enhanced parking to serve growing community.

Expansion Phase

2015 - Modernization

Integration of modern amenities including audio-visual systems, climate control, and accessibility features.

Modernization

2020 - Digital Integration

Implementation of digital infrastructure for online services, virtual events, and enhanced community engagement.

Digital Upgrade
