Daily & Monthly Spiritual Ceremonies

Regular Poojas such as Raghu Kalam, Amavasya, Pournami, Chaturthi, Pradosam, Ashtami, Karthigai, Chithirai, Vishagam & Swathi Nakshatram are conducted on a daily and monthly basis. Annadhanam is provided every day and all devotees are requested to participate.

Sankata Hara Chaturthi

Special abhishekam to Lord Pancha Mukha Heramba Maha Ganapathy and Kamala Siddhi Vinayakar.

10:30 AM

Krishna Paksha Ashtami

Special abhishekam to Goddess Asta Dasa Bhuja Durga Parameswari followed by alankaram and deeparadanai.

Abhishekam 10:30 AM
Navavarna 6:00 PM

Pradosham

Grand celebration for Sri Mahasahasralingamurthy and Lord Nandikeswarar with Mahanyasam and special abhishekam.

Mahanyasam 5:00 PM
Abhishekam 5:30 PM
Deeparadanai 6:30 PM

Special abhishekam to Goddess Prathiyankara Devi and Lord Pancha Mukha Anjaneyar followed by alankaram.

10:00 AM

Special abhishekam to Lord Swaminatha Swami with traditional rituals and offerings.

10:00 AM
